Flower Ice Bowl

Ingredients:

Edible flowers.
You could choose a mixture of flowers or just one type, such as rose petals.
Two bowls:
Put water in the smaller bowl and leave in the freezer overnight.

Preparation

Wash the flowers and pat dry.
Fill the bowl half full with water using a pitcher to pour.
Place the flowers across the top of the water so that it's completely covered.
Place a smaller bowl over the flowers in the bowl and gently let it drop to the base of the larger bowl.
The water should shift up the side gap, which will be about ½ to 1 inch (1 to 2.5cm) in thickness.
The flowers will be caught both underneath and along the sides of the bowl as well;
add more flowers to the gap, pushing them in with the wooden skewer.
It can be as packed with flowers as you like, or left more sparse.

Put freezer tape across the bowls to hold them in place.
Put the bowl arrangement into the freezer for 30 minutes.

Remove from the freezer and add more flowers inside the gap, if needed.
Or, simply reposition the flowers if they've slipped in directions that aren't so aesthetically pleasing.
If adding more flowers to already frozen edges, wet them a little for added stickiness.

Place back in the freezer.
It's recommended that you check a few more times to reposition flowers or rounds as needed.
Then, leave to freeze overnight.

Remove from the freezer the next day.
Take off the tape and leave on a towel for a few minutes.
It might come loose on its own.
If not, fill the smaller bowl with lukewarm water and gently ease it out.
Remove the outer bowl.
If it hasn't already loosened while sitting on the towels, dip it into a basin with a little lukewarm water.
Twist the ice bowl to loosen it and gently pull it out.
Place the ice bowl onto its own platter or stand.
Return to the freezer until needed.
If you're keeping it in the freezer, wrap it in kitchen foil first to prevent sticking to the freezer or other frozen items.
Serve. There are several things you can do with the ice bowl, such as:

  • ❀Arrange a fruit salad or single fruit such as hulled strawberries.
  • ❀Fill with a garden salad full of edible flowers.
  • ❀Add ice cubes and stick ice confectionery on sticks into the ice cubes.
  • ❀Add ice cubes and a few small cans of drinks.
  • ❀Use a container for dips.
  • ❀Arrange cooked seafood, such as prawns, shrimps, etc. that need to be kept cool.
  • ❀Use for a cold soup such as a gazpacho.
  • ❀And saving the best till last–– fill with ice cream!
