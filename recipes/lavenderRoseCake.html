Lavender Rose Cake

Ingredients:

Batter
½ cup (125 ml) unbleached all-purpose flour
A pinch of salt
½ cup (125 ml) unsalted butter, softened
¾ cup (180 ml) sugar
1 cup (250 ml) ground almonds
2 tbsp (30 ml) amaretto liqueur
4 eggs
icing sugar, for rolling
Icing
1 tbsp icing sugar/sugar
½ teaspoon dried lavender

Preparation

With the rack in the middle position, preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F). Generously brush four 250 ml (1 cup) rose-shaped cake moulds or any other moulds with butter.
In a bowl, combine the flour and salt. Set aside.
In another bowl, cream the butter and sugar with an electric mixer. Add the ground almonds and amaretto and stir to blend. Add the eggs, one at a time, beating until smooth. On low speed, add the dry ingredients. Divide the batter among the moulds. Bake for about 30 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the centre of the cake comes out clean. Unmould immediately. Cool completely on a wire rack.
In a coffee grinder or mortar, grind the sugar and lavender into a fine powder. With a small sieve, dust the lavender sugar over the cooled cakes.

Note:
The rose-shaped cake pan is sold under the NordicWare brand in most cookware stores.
