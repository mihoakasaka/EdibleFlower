Lavender Sables

Ingredients:

½ cup butter
½ cup brown sugar
1 egg beaten
1 tbsp lavender
1½ cups flour
1 tsp baking powder

Preparation

Preheat the oven to 350ºF/180ºC.
Cream the butter and sugar together until smooth.
Beat in the egg. Stir through the lavender.
Sift the flour and baking powder and beat in.
Roll balls and flatten on a cookie sheet. See note.
Pick the top with the tines of a fork in attractive patterns.
Bake 15 to 20 minutes until browned on top and cooked through.
Cool on wire racks.

Note:
If you prefer perfect round sables,
roll the dough into a log, chill in the freezer until firm,
slice into rounds, and lay them on the sheet.
