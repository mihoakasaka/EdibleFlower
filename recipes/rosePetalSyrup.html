Rose Petal Syrup

Ingredients:

1 cup agave
1 cup water
3 roses

Preparation

Give your roses a little rinse and then add all three ingredients to a saucepan.

Simmer gently for about 15 minutes.

Turn off the heat and allow to cool. The longer you leave the rose petals in, the more intense the flavor of your syrup will be.

After a couple of hours (or even overnight), strain the petals out and pop your syrup in a container where it will live happily in the fridge for a month or so.

This syrup is marvelous for drizzling over cakes and even more delicious splashed into a whole range of summer cocktails. How about with champagne?
